# KPIs — Leverage Media

> Owned by analytics-officer. The CEO reads the weekly report; agents read this file
> to know what "results" means. Stage 1 targets (AUDIENCE stage).

## North star
**True subscribers**: newsletter subscribers who opened ≥2 of the last 4 issues.
Target: 1,000 within 6 months of first publish.

## Company-level KPIs
| Metric | Target | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| True subscribers | +8%/week | weekly |
| Newsletter open rate | ≥ 45% | per issue |
| Newsletter CTR | ≥ 6% | per issue |
| Blog: organic clicks | +10%/week after week 8 | weekly |
| Social: profile-visit→subscribe rate | ≥ 3% | weekly |
| Pipeline throughput | 1 newsletter + 2 essays + 12 social posts/week | weekly |
| Pipeline quality | 0 pieces shipped with a gate skipped | always |

## Department KPIs (used by performance-coach for context, not as sole scores)
- Research: dossier→brief acceptance rate ≥ 80% (strategist doesn't send dossiers back)
- Production: red-team survival within 2 rounds ≥ 70%
- Quality: published corrections required: 0
- Growth: every published piece has a rollout plan within 24h
- Business: weekly report shipped every Monday, includes ≥3 actionable insights

## Measurement honesty rules
- No platform data connected yet → metrics marked `[PENDING REAL DATA]`. Agents never invent numbers.
- Once publishing starts, the CEO connects: newsletter platform stats, X/LinkedIn analytics,
  Google Search Console. analytics-officer ingests exports from `company/data/`.
- Internal metrics (throughput, gate pass rates, scores) are real from day one — they come from company/ files.
