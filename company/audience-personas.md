# Audience Personas — Leverage Media

> Owned by audience-analyst. Updated as evidence accumulates (engagement data, replies, research).
> Version: v1 (founding hypothesis — flagged for validation by real data)

## P1 — "Escape-Velocity Employee" (primary, est. 55%)
- 28–42, mid/senior in tech/marketing/ops. Salary comfortable, autonomy zero.
- Has 1–3 side-project false starts. Time budget: 6–10 hrs/week.
- **Pain:** drowning in AI content, starving for an actual sequence of steps.
- **Hires us to:** compress research, give him a system he can run nights/weekends.
- **Wins when:** first $1 online, first 100 subscribers, first automated workflow that holds.
- **Content fit:** Playbooks, Build in public. Tone: respect his intelligence, protect his time.

## P2 — "Solo Operator" (est. 30%)
- 30–50, already runs a one-person business (freelance, agency-of-one, small SaaS, creator).
- **Pain:** she IS the bottleneck. Revenue capped by her hours.
- **Hires us to:** replace parts of herself with systems/agents without quality collapse.
- **Wins when:** a deliverable ships without her touching it; revenue grows while hours don't.
- **Content fit:** Teardowns, Tool truth. Tone: peer-to-peer, operator to operator. She smells fluff instantly.

## P3 — "AI-Curious Builder" (est. 15%)
- 22–30, technical or semi-technical. Builds for the craft; business is the excuse.
- **Pain:** can build anything, ships nothing; no distribution instincts.
- **Hires us to:** show what's worth building and how distribution actually works.
- **Content fit:** Build in public, Playbooks with technical depth. Will share aggressively when impressed → growth lever.

## Resonance heuristics (what historically works in this niche)
1. Real numbers in the headline outperform curiosity gaps for P1/P2.
2. "How I/we did X" >> "How to do X" — first-person evidence is the moat.
3. Negative tool verdicts earn disproportionate trust and shares.
4. P2 converts to paid first; P3 drives top-of-funnel reach; P1 is volume.

## Anti-persona
Get-rich-quick seekers wanting passive income without operating anything. We do not write for them;
their engagement is a vanity metric that poisons the feedback loop.
