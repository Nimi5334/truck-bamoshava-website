# Brand Bible — Leverage Media

> THE source of truth for what this company is. Every agent reads this before producing anything.
> Changing this file pivots the entire company.

## Identity
- **Name:** Leverage Media
- **Flagship newsletter:** *The Leverage Letter*
- **One-liner:** We show ambitious individuals how to build a real business alone, using AI as their workforce.
- **Niche:** AI-powered solopreneurship — systems, tools, and playbooks for one-person businesses.
- **Founder/CEO:** Nimrod (the only human in the company).

## Positioning
We are NOT another "10 AI tools you missed this week" account. We publish **operator-grade
playbooks**: what we actually ran, what it cost, what broke, what it returned. The company
itself — 24 agents run by one person — is the living proof and the best recurring story.

**Enemy we define ourselves against:** AI-hype grifters who sell screenshots of tools they never used.

## Audience promise
Every piece must pass this test: *"Can a smart, busy person apply this within 7 days
and tell that something changed?"* If not, it doesn't ship.

## Content pillars (every piece maps to exactly one)
1. **Playbooks** — step-by-step systems with real numbers ("How our 24-agent pipeline writes a newsletter").
2. **Teardowns** — how a real one-person business works (revenue, stack, hours).
3. **Tool truth** — hands-on verdicts, including negative ones. We say when things suck.
4. **Build in public** — Leverage Media's own metrics, failures, and experiments.

## Voice (summary — full rules in style-guide.md)
Direct, specific, slightly irreverent, zero hype. We write like an operator texting a
smart friend, not like a brand. Numbers over adjectives. Verbs over buzzwords.

## Business model (current stage: AUDIENCE)
- Stage 1 (now): grow newsletter to 1,000 true subscribers. No monetization pressure on content.
- Stage 2 (1k–10k): sponsorships (1/week max), affiliate only for tools we verifiably use.
- Stage 3 (10k+): paid product — templates/playbooks vault or cohort.
- Hard rule: we never promote a tool we haven't used. Trust is the only asset.

## Channels
- Newsletter (flagship, weekly, Thursday)
- Blog/SEO (2 essays/week, evergreen)
- X/Twitter (daily), LinkedIn (3x/week), YouTube scripts (1/week when ready)
